Our Mission

Connecting the Baton of Value to the World

Grand Trading Co., Ltd. aims to provide excellent domestic second-hand goods and traditional crafts to the world through a fair market.
We embody the Japanese spirit of cherishing old and good things ("Mottainai") through business,
and contribute to the realization of a recycling-oriented society and the inheritance of Japanese culture.
