Message from the CEO

Creating a world full of "Smiles"

I started my business at the age of 20 and am now in my 6th year.
In a world where I didn't know left from right, I have made many mistakes and experienced life-changing decisions many times.

My generation is facing increasingly difficult living conditions with lower wages and rising consumption taxes.
In such an environment, I feel that there are few people who challenge themselves, and many who have no dreams or hope.

As a representative of my 20s in such a world, I want to give even a little hope and a spirit of challenge, and create a world full of "smiles".

CEO
Kyosuke Onuki
