Message from the CEO

Connecting "Unwanted" and "Needed" — Delivering Made in Japan to the World

Our company focuses on discovering new value in items that are still usable and connecting them to the world through recycling and overseas export.

Even products that are considered to have finished their roles in Japan have people who need them in other parts of the world. Connecting the "unwanted" with the "needed"—this is our mission.

Throughout my career, I have seen many instances where perfectly functional products were being discarded. Every time I witnessed this, I couldn't help but wonder, "Is this truly the right way?"

Resources are finite, and every item inherently possesses value.

We are more than just an exporter. As a participant in the resource circulation cycle, we strive to remain a bridge between Japan and the rest of the world.

We promise to continue being a sincere and reliable partner, taking responsibility for every single transaction and working with dedication and persistence.

CEO
Kyosuke Osawa
