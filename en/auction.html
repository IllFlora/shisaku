BtoB Live Auction

Grand Auction operates from six bases in Chiba, Nagano, Mie, Ibaraki, Yamanashi, and Fukushima, receiving products from various businesses across Japan.
Currently, many listings come from waste collection services, second-hand shops, and purchasing stores, primarily handling used goods. The LIVE format allows real-time participation from anywhere via smartphone or PC.
Please feel free to contact us if you are interested in listing or participating.
