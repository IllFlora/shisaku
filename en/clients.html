Meeting Diverse Needs

Grand Trading Co., Ltd. develops purchase and reuse businesses that respond to the needs of a wide range of customers, from government/municipalities, corporations, traders, to individuals.
We propose the optimal plan tailored to the characteristics of each and contribute to the realization of a sustainable society.

Government / Municipalities

Please consider reusing bulky waste overseas.

Among the general bulky waste brought into cleaning factories, there are many items that can still be used overseas.
Instead of crushing and burying them in landfills, please let us reuse them overseas.

We ask you to separate and store items that are collected from general households at each municipal cleaning center and are usually put into the crusher.
When the goods accumulate at the storage location, please contact us, and our collection staff will come for inspection and collection.

To confirm, we properly dispose of items that cannot be reused overseas after collection in accordance with laws and regulations.

Transaction Flow
  • 01 Inquiry

    Please feel free to contact us first.

  • 02 Hearing / Inspection

    We will check the current status of the waste and the storage location.

  • 03 Proposal / Contract

    We will propose collection items and schemes and conclude a contract.

  • 04 Separation / Collection

    Please store them separately at the designated location, and we will visit regularly for collection.

Corporations

Do you have inventory that is difficult to sell in Japan?

We purchase reusable home appliances, tableware, furniture, miscellaneous goods, etc. from mass retailers, rental companies, recycle shops, etc.
Even disused items that have no value in Japan are in demand in developing countries and are repaired and sold overseas.

"They are treasures for those who cannot buy new ones." In order to deliver them to such people, please let us utilize your dead stock.

Transaction Flow
  • 01 Inquiry

    Please let us know the type and amount of inventory.

  • 02 Quotation

    We will check the product list or the actual items and present a purchase price.

  • 03 Carry-out / Collection

    We will carry out the removal work on the specified date.

  • 04 Payment

    After completion, we will pay by cash or bank transfer.

Traders

On-the-spot assessment, immediate cash payment

Air conditioning/electrical construction contractors, moving companies, estate liquidation companies, etc.
We handle a wide range of items from a single appliance to large-volume bring-ins. Please bring them in.
We will assess the items you bring in on the spot and pay in cash immediately.

Air conditioning contractors

We purchase air conditioners, cables, pipes, etc. generated during repairs or replacements.

Electrical work contractors

We purchase electric wires, component scraps, old wire stock, etc. that are no longer needed after construction.

Moving companies

We purchase a wide range of items such as unnecessary home appliances, tableware, furniture, and general goods generated during moving.

Estate liquidation companies

We purchase a wide range of items such as unnecessary home appliances, tableware, furniture, and general goods generated during organization.

Transaction Flow
  • 01 Bring-in

    Please bring the items during business hours (no reservation required).

  • 02 Assessment

    Our specialized staff will assess them speedily on the spot.

  • 03 Cash Purchase

    If you agree with the assessed amount, we will pay you in cash on the spot.

Individuals

Please consult us about things that cannot be bought by recycle shops.

We purchase home appliances, bicycles, tableware, furniture, miscellaneous goods, etc. that are no longer needed at home due to estate liquidation, moving, or decluttering.

We may be able to handle items that cannot be purchased by general recycle shops.
We will assess the items you bring in on the spot and pay in cash immediately.

Transaction Flow
  • 01 Inquiry / Visit

    Please call us first. Walk-ins are also welcome.

  • 02 Assessment

    We will check the condition of the items and present an assessed amount.

  • 03 Purchase / Payment

    We will pay in cash on the spot.
